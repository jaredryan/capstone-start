How it works:

1. Enter a keyword and search for a movie or TV show.

2. If you don't see your results, hit the Try Again button until it appears.

(The servers involved are not the most reliable.)

3. Hover or click on what you want to learn more about.

4. If you like what you see, click Add to List.

5. When you are done, click on Go to Your List to see your choices.
